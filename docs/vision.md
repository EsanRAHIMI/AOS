# Vision

Build the core engine of a future autonomous AI operating system: an autonomous
agentic factory kernel that can think, plan, execute, monitor, report, and
improve — coordinating many independent AI agents and services, developing new
tools, learning from its own actions, and giving its owner full visibility and
control through a real-time dashboard.

The kernel is the beginning of an autonomous company operating system and,
eventually, a digital extension of the owner's decision-making and execution.

## Non-negotiables
- Production-grade from day one; modular, observable, scalable.
- Independent services with clear boundaries and strong contracts.
- Documentation and memory as first-class citizens (cheap context for future agents).
- Human approval for sensitive actions; autonomy that is observable and auditable.
- Latest stable technologies; no experimental or abandoned dependencies.
